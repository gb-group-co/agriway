AGRIWAY, a brand from GB group


Since 1902, Gregoire-Besson is specialist of tillage equipment and has become a worldwide leading company.

With 5 factories in France, 1 in Germany and 1 in Italy, Gregoire-Besson is exporting implements in more than 65 countries and the quality of its products is renowned all over the farming world.

As part of its development, the group decided in 2009 to create a new range of products dedicated to tractors between 40HP and 140HP.

By this brand the target is to offer simple implements but with a strong focus on quality and reliability.
